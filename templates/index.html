This App is intended to replace the manual input for https://www.stadtradeln.de/


Step 1:



This App needs the permission to read the activities information, in order to get the number of kilometers.
What happens with your data you can see here


Step 2:

Add your credentials for Stadtradeln


Your credentials are encrypted and saved in mongodb atlas here
And are used to login here, when new Strava activity is being created.
